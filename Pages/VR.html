VR/AR

An immersive, high-fidelity virtual training system developed for the Iranian Airports Holding Company to bridge real-world operations with interactive simulation.

Project Overview

This project was commissioned to solve a critical training challenge: providing air traffic controllers with a risk-free, highly realistic environment to practice complex radar tracking, communication protocols, and emergency management. Built from scratch in Unity, the system processes multi-agent data feeds to simulate crowded airspace conditions accurately.

Key Challenges & Solutions

  • Real-time Performance: Managing dozens of active aircraft telemetry inputs simultaneously. Solved using optimized C# data structures and physics stripping.
  • Cross-Platform UI: Ensuring the simulation data looks precise on high-res PC monitors and remains responsive on Android tablets.
